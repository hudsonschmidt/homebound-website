How It Works

1

Create Your Trip

Choose your activity, set your destination with our enhanced location search, and pick your expected return time. Going with friends? Create a group trip and invite them along.

2

Add Safety Contacts

Select emergency contacts from your saved list or add friends who use Homebound. Friends get push notifications while others receive detailed emails with your trip info.

3

Head Out

Start your trip and watch the countdown timer on your home screen, lock screen, or Dynamic Island. Check in during your adventure to let contacts know you're okay.

4

Return Safely

Tap "I'm Safe" when you're back. Running late? Extend your time with one tap. If you don't check out, your contacts are automatically notified with your location details.

Built for Adventurers

Whether you're hitting the trail or heading out on the water, Homebound has you covered.

Features

Activity-Specific Trips

Choose from hiking, climbing, diving, cycling, and more. Each activity comes with tailored safety tips and messaging.

Smart Notifications

Your emergency contacts receive detailed trip info—location, notes, and activity type—if you're overdue. Critical alerts break through Focus and Do Not Disturb and appear on CarPlay, so they're never missed.

Safety Score & Weekly Recap

Monitor your safety habits with a personal score based on on-time rates, check-ins, and trip completions. Review weekly recaps of your adventure hours and activity. All computed on-device for privacy.

Group Trips

Plan adventures with up to 20 friends. Everyone can check in and finish on their own time, add people after a trip starts, and watch each other live on one combined map.

Live Location

Share your real-time GPS position with safety contacts during active trips. Contacts can follow your progress on a map for added peace of mind.

Circles

Share your live location with exactly the friends you choose—for a day, 3 days, or a week. See everyone who's sharing with you, live, on your map, and stop anytime.

Live Activity

See your active trip countdown on your lock screen and Dynamic Island. Check in and mark yourself safe without unlocking your phone.

Flexible Check-In

Check in via the app or through simple web links. No login required for your contacts to see your status.

Grace Periods

Set a buffer time before overdue alerts trigger. Life happens—a few extra minutes won't cause a false alarm.

Plan From Anywhere

Create trips even without a signal—they activate the moment you reconnect. Trips detect your time zone automatically, so your ETA is always right.

Friends

Connect with fellow adventurers. Share trips, see when friends are out exploring, and request check-in updates from each other.

Achievements

Earn 40+ badges and milestones as you complete trips. Track your progress from First Trip to Weekend Warrior and beyond.

Trip History & Search

Browse and search your past adventures. View statistics, revisit trip details, and track your progress over time.

Trip Map

See all your adventures plotted on an interactive map. Visualize where you've been and discover patterns in your explorations.

Discover

Find places and activities around you with current conditions—from trailheads and climbing spots to concerts nearby.

Enhanced Location Search

Find trails, parks, and points of interest with distance info. Select exact locations on the map for precise trip planning.

Widgets

Quick access to your trips right from your home screen. Start trips and check in without opening the app.

Trip Saving

Save your favorite routes and locations. Quickly recreate past trips without re-entering all the details.

Data Export

Export your trips, contacts, and profile data anytime. Your data belongs to you.

Adventure with Confidence

A 10-second check-in can become a lifesaving signal. Download Homebound and give your loved ones peace of mind.
