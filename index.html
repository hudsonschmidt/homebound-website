The whole trip, covered

Before. During. After.

Before you go
Make the plan in under a minute.
  • Pick from 25+ activities with tailored safety tips
  • Search trails, parks and points of interest, or drop a pin
  • Invite up to 20 friends to a group trip
  • Save favorite routes for one-tap reuse
  • No signal? Plan offline. It activates when you reconnect
While you're out
Stay visible to the people who care.
  • Live location on one combined map
  • Check in with a tap from the lock screen or Dynamic Island
  • Contacts follow along on the web, no login needed
  • Circles: always-on sharing for a day, 3 days, or a week
  • SOS button when you need help now
When you're back
One tap ends the worry.
  • Tap "I'm Safe" and everyone knows you're home
  • Running late? Extend your ETA with one tap
  • Safety Score and weekly recap, computed on-device
  • 40+ achievements from First Trip to Weekend Warrior
  • Searchable history and a map of everywhere you've been

…and if you're ever overdue, your contacts automatically get your location, notes, and activity. Alerts break through Do Not Disturb.
